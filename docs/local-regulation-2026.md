## Why This Matters

Drug approval by the FDA or EMA is only **step one** — market access (pricing, reimbursement, HTA) varies dramatically by jurisdiction and can determine whether a drug is commercially viable. This document provides an overview of the key local regulatory frameworks every pipeline analyst must consider.

## 🇺🇸 USA — Inflation Reduction Act (IRA) 2026

**The most significant US pharma pricing reform in decades.**

### Medicare Part D Price Negotiation

- **Effective:** September 1, 2026
- **First 10 drugs:** Eliquis, Jardiance, Xarelto, Januvia, Farxiga, Entresto, Enbrel, Imbruvica, Stelara, Fiasp/NovoLog
- **Combined Medicare spend (2022):** ~$50.2B
- **Expected Medicare savings:** ~$6B (2026), rising to ~$15B (2031)
- **Average discount off list price:** 35–78%

### The Small Molecule Penalty

| Modality | Years Until Medicare Negotiation |
|----------|--------------------------------|
| **Small Molecule** | **7 years** |
| **Biologic** | **13 years** |
| **Orphan-Only Drug** | **Excluded** |

**Consequence:** ~22% drop in Phase 2 small-molecule trials for non-orphan indications since 2022; ~14% increase in biologics (IQVIA 2025).

### Key Dates 2026

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| **Sep 1, 2026** | First negotiated prices take effect |
| **Mar 2026** | Second negotiation round starts (15 more Part D drugs) |
| **Nov 2026** | Part B drugs (physician-administered) included for first time |

### Orphan Drug Exclusion

Drugs with a single orphan-designated indication are excluded from negotiation. If the drug later gains a non-orphan indication, **all sales** become eligible for negotiation.

## 🇩🇪 Germany — AMNOG System

Germany's **Arzneimittelmarkt-Neuordnungsgesetz (AMNOG)** has been the most influential EU pricing model since 2011.

### Process (standard ~12–15 months post-launch)

1. Manufacturer sets launch price freely (Year 1)
2. Dossier submitted to G-BA within 4 months
3. IQWiG evaluates added benefit vs. appropriate comparator therapy (3 months)
4. G-BA issues benefit category resolution (3 months)
5. Price negotiation with GKV-Spitzenverband (6 months; 3 more if arbitration)

### Benefit Categories (2026 Reform — Fixed Effect Sizes)

| Category | New Threshold (2026) |
|----------|---------------------|
| **Major** | ≥50% RRR in mortality, or cure |
| **Considerable** | 25–50% RRR, or ≥0.5 SMD on PROMs |
| **Minor** | 15–25% RRR, or 0.2–0.5 SMD |
| **Non-quantifiable** | Insufficient data |
| **No added benefit** | <15% RRR or below MCID |

### Key Cases

- **Leqembi (lecanemab):** "No added benefit" → price tied to Alzheimer standard therapy (~€1,000–3,000/yr vs $26,500 US)
- **Dupixent (COPD):** Likely "considerable" added benefit (34% RRR in exacerbations)

### Orphan Drugs

Automatic added benefit presumption only for sales <€50M/year. Above threshold → full AMNOG assessment.

## 🇫🇷 France — HAS/CEPS System

### SMR (Medical Benefit) → Determines Reimbursement

| Level | Meaning | Rate |
|-------|---------|------|
| Important (I) | Major benefit | 65% |
| Moderate (II) | Moderate benefit | 30% |
| Low (III) | Low benefit | 15% |
| Insufficient (IV) | Not listed | 0% |

### ASMR (Added Clinical Value) → Determines Price

- **ASMR I:** Revolutionary (first-in-class) — highest price
- **ASMR V:** No improvement — **56% of all new drugs** (2024 data)
- Drugs with ASMR V get minimal price premium over existing generics

### Key Characteristics

- **No statutory max for CEPS negotiation** → average 400–600 days from EU approval to patient access
- **Safeguard clause (clause de sauvegarde):** Industry-wide rebates when drug spending exceeds cap
- **Early Access (AAP):** For drugs with presumed ASMR I–III, Phase 2+ data required
- Total pharma payback: significant pressure on net pricing

## 🇮🇹 Italy — AIFA System

### Complexities

- **21 regional formularies** — up to 6–12 month delay between national approval and regional availability
- **Registri AIFA:** ~210 mandatory patient monitoring registries; 12–18% of patients fail eligibility criteria
- **Payback system:** ~€2.3B cumulative industry payback owed (2013–2024). Pharma companies pay 50% of regional drug budget overruns

### Timelines

- Innovative drugs: 6–9 months from EU approval to reimbursement
- Standard drugs: 12–24 months
- 5-year re-evaluation cycle for all reimbursed drugs

## 🇬🇧 UK — MHRA/NICE Post-Brexit

### MHRA Independence

- Separate regulator since Jan 2021; International Recognition Framework (2024) accepts FDA/EMA/PMDA reviews
- **ILAP (Innovation Passport):** 80+ products designated, integrates regulatory + HTA + NHS access in one pathway
- Rolling review and 150-day accelerated assessment available

### NICE Technology Appraisal

| Threshold | £/QALY |
|-----------|--------|
| Standard | £20,000–30,000 |
| End-of-life | up to £50,000 |
| Highly Specialised | up to £100,000–300,000 |

### Key Appraisals (2025–2026)

- **Wegovy:** Recommended (BMI ≥35 + comorbidity, 2-year limit)
- **Mounjaro:** Recommended (BMI ≥35 + comorbidity, 2025)
- **Leqembi:** Ongoing appraisal; limited recommendation for ApoE4 non-carriers expected
- **Tirzepatide cardiovascular:** New data influencing broader access

## 🇯🇵 Japan — PMDA/NHI System

### Review Times

- **Standard:** 12 months target; ~9–11 months actual for NMEs
- **Sakigake (Breakthrough):** 6–9 months; 40+ products designated
- Drug lag reduced from 3–7 years to 6–18 months

### NHI Pricing (Yakka)

- **Biennial price revision:** Average 5–9% cut every 2 years
- **Initial pricing:** Similarity-based premium (up to +50%) or foreign average price method
- **HTA (since 2019):** ¥5–7.5M/QALY threshold; price reduction up to 50% for drugs above threshold

## 🇨🇳 China — NMPA/NRDL System

### NRDL Annual Negotiation (Most Aggressive Pricing System)

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Average price cut | ~61% (2024) |
| ICER threshold | 1–3× GDP/capita (~$12K–36K/QALY) |
| Drugs on NRDL (2024) | ~3,088 |
| Cumulative savings (2018–2023) | ~¥500B (~$70B) |

### Key Features

- **Annual NRDL re-negotiation** — drugs re-priced every January
- **PD-1 price war:** 8+ domestic PD-1 inhibitors → ~70% discount
- **Breakthrough Therapy (since 2020):** 120+ drugs; 100-working-day review target
- **ICH convergence:** Full adoption of CTD, MedDRA, GCP, MRCT guidelines
